ISRO/DRDO Scientist Exam Preparation

Introduction

The ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation) and DRDO (Defence Research and Development Organisation) Scientist exams are prestigious examinations conducted to recruit scientists and engineers in various disciplines for India's premier space and defense research organizations.

These exams offer exciting opportunities for engineers and scientists to contribute to India's space and defense programs, working on cutting-edge technologies and research projects.

Exam Details

Organization Exam Name Eligibility Selection Process
ISRO ISRO Scientist/Engineer Exam BE/B.Tech or equivalent in relevant discipline with 65% marks Written Test + Interview
DRDO DRDO Scientist Entry Test (SET) Master's degree in relevant subject or BE/B.Tech in engineering Written Test + Interview

Exam Pattern

The examination pattern typically consists of:

  • Technical Section: Questions from your core engineering/scientific discipline
  • Aptitude Section: Quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, and verbal ability
  • Research Aptitude: For higher positions, questions may assess research methodology

Syllabus Highlights

The syllabus varies based on the engineering discipline or scientific field, but generally includes:

For Electronics & Communication Engineers:

  • Analog and Digital Electronics
  • Communication Systems
  • Signals and Systems
  • Control Systems
  • Electromagnetic Theory

For Mechanical Engineers:

  • Thermodynamics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Machine Design
  • Manufacturing Processes
  • Heat Transfer

Preparation Strategy

  1. Understand the Syllabus: Thoroughly go through the official syllabus for your discipline.
  2. Core Subject Mastery: Focus on your core engineering/scientific subjects as they carry maximum weightage.
  3. Previous Year Papers: Solve at least 5-10 years of previous question papers.
  4. Aptitude Practice: Dedicate time daily for quantitative aptitude and reasoning.
  5. Technical Updates: Stay updated with recent developments in your field and ISRO/DRDO projects.
  6. Mock Tests: Take regular mock tests to improve speed and accuracy.

Important Topics for General Aptitude

  • Number System and Simplification
  • Percentage, Profit and Loss
  • Time, Speed and Distance
  • Time and Work
  • Ratio and Proportion
  • Logical Reasoning
  • Verbal Ability
